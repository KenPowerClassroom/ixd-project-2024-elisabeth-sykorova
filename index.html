Introduction

Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design project, focus on gamifying a learning app.
Our aim is to make learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game contexts, such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement and enjoyment.

This project consists of a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.
